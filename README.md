## How to Use

1. **Add Tasks**: Click "+ Add Item" in any section to create new tasks
2. **Check Off Tasks**: Click on tasks to mark them complete
3. **Complete Your Day**: Once all tasks are checked, click "Complete Day" to increment your streak
4. **Build Your Streak**: Complete all tasks every day to maintain your streak

## How It Works

- **Daily Reset**: Checkboxes automatically reset each new day
- **Streak Logic**: Your streak increases when you complete all tasks on consecutive days
- **Streak Break**: If you skip a day, your streak resets to 0
- **Data Persistence**: All your data is saved locally in your browser

## Customization

You can easily customize:
- Section names (click on them to edit)
- Task names (click on any task to edit)
- Add or remove tasks as needed

## Technical Details

- Pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript - no dependencies
